AI Training Game for Autonomous Robot in Complex Interactive Environment


复杂交互环境下自主机器人AI训练游戏


By: Chen Guoyi, license under Apache 2.0 & CC BY-NC-ND 4.0.

欢迎您游玩复杂交互环境下自主机器人AI训练游戏。在这个游戏中，您将操控一个机器人在充满随机运动的障碍物的仿射惯性系中穿梭。您的目标是安全操控机器人到达目的地，并尽可能地避免与运动中障碍物的距离过近。
Welcome to play the AI Training Game for Autonomous Robots in Complex Interactive Environments. In this game, you will control a robot navigating through a simulated inertial system filled with randomly moving obstacles. Your goal is to safely guide the robot to its destination while avoiding getting too close to the moving obstacles.

一旦您成功到达目的地，您的浏览器将自动为您下载一份csv文件，里面包含了机器人的运动学数据。请您将这份csv文件（或者多份文件一起打包为zip格式）发到我在新加坡国立大学计算机系的工作邮箱: Guoyi@comp.nus.edu.sg，邮件标题为：[UROP]+您的名字。一旦您的csv文件被我们用于训练真实的机器人，您的名字将会出现在我们的网页或论文的致谢中。
Once you successfully reach the destination, your browser will automatically download a CSV file containing the kinematic data of the robot. Please send this CSV file (or compress files in one .zip file) to my work email at the School of Computing @ National University of Singapore, Guoyi@comp.nus.edu.sg, with the email subject: [UROP]+Your Name. Once your CSV file is used to train real robots, your name will appear in the acknowledgments section of our webpage or paper.

您是NUS的学生或者教职工吗？请您通过表单快速提交。
Are you a student or staff member at NUS? Please submit the file through the form.



最低系统要求

Minimum System Requirements


  • 您需要使用鼠标进行游戏，本游戏目前无法支持触控设备。
  • You need to use a mouse to play the game; currently, touch devices are not supported.
  • 如果您觉得游戏卡顿，请在游玩时接入外接电源。
  • If you experience any lag during the game, please connect to an external power source while playing.
  • 建议使用最新版本的Chrome、Firefox等主流浏览器。
  • It is recommended to use the latest versions of mainstream browsers such as Chrome or Firefox.


操作指南

Operational Guide


您将通过鼠标操作机器人在巨大的地图上运动，在游戏页面中你只能看到有限的地图。如下图所示：
You will control the robot's movement on a large map through mouse operations. In the game page, you can only see a limited part of the map as shown in the figure below:



上图的机器人在它的视场中运行，所以机器人永远居于地图正中心。为了方便您操纵，我们用带有不同颜色的线段告知了您的机器人在全局地图上的信息，包括：
The robot in the above picture runs within its field of view, so it is always in the center of the map. For your convenience, we have used lines with different colors to inform you of your robot's information on the global map, including:

  • 红色线段：机器人指向鼠标的距离，线段越长，机器人在所指方向上的加速度越大。
  • Red line: The distance from the robot to the mouse. The longer the line, the greater the acceleration of the robot in the direction pointed.
  • 蓝色线段：机器人指向目的地（绿色方块）的距离，上标有距离数值。
  • Blue line: The distance from the robot to the destination (green square), with the distance value marked above.
  • 黄色线段：进入机器人危险区域的障碍物与机器人的连线，您应该避免过多的连线。
  • Yellow line: The line connecting obstacles entering the robot's danger zone to the robot. You should avoid too many connections.
