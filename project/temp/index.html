Project Scope and Description:

This internship opportunity is designed for students who possess a robust understanding of their core disciplines—specifically Computer Engineering or Electrical Engineering—alongside a thorough grounding in Physics. The project focuses on leveraging these interdisciplinary skills to design, develop, and implement sophisticated electrical control units. These units will be pivotal in conducting advanced physics experiments, particularly those involving the heat processing of materials.

Intern's Responsibilities:

As an intern, you will take on the role of an Electrical Engineer, where your primary responsibilities will include:

  • Designing Electrical Control Units: Develop detailed designs for electrical control units that meet the specific requirements of our physics experiments. This involves schematic creation, component selection, and simulation of electrical circuits to ensure reliability and efficiency.
  • Deployment and Testing: Oversee the deployment of these control units within the experimental setup. This includes constructing the units, integrating them with existing systems, and conducting rigorous testing to ensure they operate as intended under various conditions.
  • Collaboration and Innovation: Work closely with a team of engineers and physicists to ensure the seamless integration of electrical control units with the experimental apparatus. You'll also be encouraged to propose innovative solutions to enhance system performance and efficiency.
  • Documentation: Maintain comprehensive documentation of all design processes, deployment procedures, and testing outcomes. This documentation will be crucial for future reference and for the continuous improvement of experimental setups.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Currently pursuing or have completed a degree in Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or a related field.
  • Strong theoretical and practical knowledge of Physics, particularly in areas relevant to the project scope.
  • Experience with electrical circuit design software and tools.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and a keen eye for detail.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a multidisciplinary team.

This internship is an excellent opportunity for students to apply their academic knowledge in a practical setting, contribute to cutting-edge research, and gain valuable experience in electrical engineering and physics research.
