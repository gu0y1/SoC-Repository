A Byteville Adventure

In the digital realm of Byteville, there's an ancient computer that only grants access to its vast knowledge library during certain times of the year, based on a person's birth month. A curious student named Jamie has discovered this computer but to gain access, Jamie must correctly enter a code. This code is calculated using a unique algorithm that takes Jamie's birth month (an integer from 1 to 12) as input.

The algorithm works as follows:

  • Divide the birth month by 3 and take the ceiling of the result. The ceiling of a number is the smallest integer greater than or equal to that number.
  • If the result is greater than 4, subtract 4 from it until the result is less than or equal to 4.
