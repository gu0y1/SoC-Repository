About Tutorial

This tutorial is created to help you learn the Robot Operating System (ROS) on your own. Our goal is for future computer engineers like you to not only understand how ROS works but also use what you learn here to address problems in our classes and in the real world.

This tutorial offers step-by-step solutions complete with detailed debugging instructions, ensuring you not only understand what to do but also how to do it effectively.

The success of this tutorial is closely linked to the lecture notes and lab instructions from the CG2111A course, as well as the valuable feedback from CG2111A students. These resources are indispensable for a comprehensive learning experience.

We hope you find joy and enrichment in acquiring new knowledge with us. Wishing you all the best as you navigate through the final challenges of CG2111A.
